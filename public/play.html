How to Play PuzlPix

Select a Level, Mode, and picture and hit play!

Reorder the puzzle back to its original picture by swiping squares.

Levels

Make the game as challenging as you want by selecting how many rows and columns it should have.

Modes

Try to beat the clock with Countdown mode or play Moves mode and try to win with the fewest moves possible.

Hints

Use hints to improve your times and minimize your moves.

100 tokens - Show what position a square should be in starting from the top left.

10 tokens - Reveal what the original picture is.

10 tokens - Swap two squares by tapping one square, then the next.

Have fun!
