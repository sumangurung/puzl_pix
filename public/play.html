How to Play PuzlPix

Select a Level, Mode, and picture and hit play! Swipe squares to rearrange the puzzle back to its original picture.

Levels

Set the game to be as simple or as challenging as you wish. Increase the challenge level as you improve.

Modes

Try to beat the clock in Countdown mode or play Moves mode and try to win with the fewest possible moves.

Challenge Friends!

Win games and challenge friends and family to compete with your puzzle solving skills.

Cheats

For faster times and fewer moves.

Show the original picture.

Swap two squares by tapping one square, then the next.

Show the ordering of squares, starting from the top left.

Share and have fun!
